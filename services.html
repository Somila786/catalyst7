Overview · How it fits together

Most studios specialise in one thing and outsource the rest. We run three layers — technology, business consultancy and production — under a single operating discipline, so the people building your software, naming your strategy and shooting your film share one channel and one clock.

Engage a single layer when you have a sharp brief — anything from a R1,500 shoot to a transformation programme. Put all three on retainer when you'd rather stop stitching agencies together. Either way you get one accountable lead and proof on a weekly clock.
