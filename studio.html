Section · 02 / Who we are

Catalyst 7 is a creative technology studio in Pretoria. We were assembled in 2025 to do one thing — close the gap between what businesses say and what they actually ship.

The studio runs across three layers — technology, consultancy, and production — under a single operating discipline. You get one partner with the texture of three teams, and none of the handoffs.

We work with operators, founders and cultural producers across Southern Africa and beyond. Most engagements start with a single conversation and end as long-running ecosystems.

Section · 04 / Operating Principles

Six rules the work has to pass before it leaves the building.

01
Positions, not briefs

We take a side and defend it in the work — not in a forty-page deck. The thesis comes first; everything else serves it.

02
Ship the system

The deliverable is working software and live work, never the slide about it. If it doesn't run in the real world, it isn't done.

03
One signature

Three layers, one accountable lead, one clock. You manage a relationship, not a stack of agencies and their handoffs.

04
Weekly proof

Every engagement shows something real every week. Momentum is the metric; we'd rather demonstrate than report.

05
In market in ninety

We move from first listen to live within ninety days. No quiet pilots, no permanent betas, no work that never sees daylight.

06
One number

Each engagement is judged on a single number, reviewed every quarter. We earn the next one — or we don't.
